Eggs with tuna and mayo

Description:

Delicious hard-boiled eggs filled with a mix of natural canned tuna and mayo

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Natural canned tuna
  • Mayo

Steps:

  1. Bring some water to a boil in an adequatly sized pot for the eggs
  2. Once boiling, submerge the eggs and cook for 10 min
  3. In the meantime, in a little bowl, add a spoon of mayonnaise and the canned tuna, to witch your eliminated the water, an mix them togheter
  4. Once the eggs are ready, take them out and stop the cooking by submerging them in cold water
  5. Peel the eggs en carefully slice them in half
  6. Extract the yolk, add it to the mix and stir to incorporate it
  7. Fill the egg halves with the mixture and serve!
