Chick Peas Burgers

Description:

Patties made with legumes, in particular with chick peas. You can personalize them however you want, by adding other ingredients!

Ingredients:

  • 180gr Chick Peas;
  • 30g parmesan cheese;
  • 30g oat flour;
  • One egg;
  • Salt, pepper, spices;

Procedure:

  1. Blend the chick peas with the egg and the spices
  2. Add the blend in a bowl and add salt pepper and parmesan cheese and other ingredients you want, then add the flour
  3. Mix everything togheter and form patties with your hands
  4. Heat a non-stick pan and place the patties on medium/low heat
