Chicken with Almonds

Description:

An incredible classic recipe from the East!

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast
  • Soy sauce
  • Peeled Almonds
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps:

  1. Cut the chicken breast in rough cubes
  2. In a pan toast the peeled almonds and put them away for the time being
  3. In the same pan pour some seed oil and, once hot, place the chicken cubes
  4. Let them sizzle for a bit, then pour a little bit of water and the soy sauce and cover with a lid
  5. Let it simmer for 5 minutes, take off the lid and add salt and pepper
  6. Add the almonds and reduce the water to your liking
  7. Plate it and serve it hot!
